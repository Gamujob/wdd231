About Us

Spear Motors Ltd is a private Limited Company duly incorporated under the Laws of Uganda. It was founded in 1973 by Prof. Gordon Wavamunno.

Spear Motors Ltd is the official distributor for:

1. Mercedes-Benz Group AG & Daimler Trucks AG of Stuttgart, Germany, the manufacturers of Mercedes Benz trucks and Mitsubishi FUSO trucks. After Sales Distributor for Mercedes-Benz PC & Vans. After Sales Distributor for FUSO trucks.

2. Stellantis, the manufacturers of Jeep and Fiat vehicles amongst many others

3 Mazda Parts and Service, which is the newest addition to the brands we represent
